The 10 Steps to Wealth

01

Master a Skill

The highest-earning players are developers. Use YouTube tutorials to learn Roblox Studio. Skilled scripters or builders can charge 5,000 to 50,000+ Robux per commission on talent hubs or Discord servers like HiddenDevs.

02

Clothing Store

Create classic shirts and pants or apply for the UGC program. While it costs 10 Robux to upload, a trending design can sell thousands of copies, providing passive income while you sleep.

03

Donation Games

Play "PLS DONATE" or "Hungry Artists." Don't just beg; offer value. Stand out by having a high-effort stand, being funny, or offering "services" like drawing avatars. Fastest way to your first 100 Robux.

04

Trading Limiteds

With Roblox Premium, you can trade "Limited" items. Buy low and sell high. Use sites like Rolimon's to track values. Requires patience and market knowledge to avoid "projected" scams.

05

Flipping Limited U

Roblox releases "Limited U" items frequently. If you buy at launch, value often triples within hours. Monitor Roblox leaks accounts on Twitter (X) for drop times.

06

GFX & Art

If you are good at Blender or Photoshop, create "GFX" (avatar renders) for thumbnails or profile pictures. High-end artists can earn thousands of Robux for a single piece of promotional art.

07

High-Retention Games

Obbies or Tycoons are easier to make. Focus on Gamepasses (2x Speed, Gravity Coils). Even a small player base can generate significant revenue through developer products.

08

Microsoft Rewards

A 100% legal way to get Robux. By using Bing and daily tasks on the Microsoft Rewards site, you earn points redeemable for official 100-1,000 Robux gift card codes.

09

Manage a Group

Start a group around a niche (fan club, military RP). Sell uniforms and ranks. Group funds are easier to manage and can be paid out to yourself or contributors instantly.

10

Reinvest Your Earnings

Do not spend your first 1,000 Robux on avatar items. Use them to:

  • Advertise your game
  • Buy Premium (Trade)
  • Hire a Specialist
  • Upload more designs

IMPORTANT RULES

1

Roblox takes a 30% cut

This applies to all sales including donations, clothing, and gamepasses. Factor this into your pricing.

2

Avoid "Free Robux" Scams

Never click links promising "Free Robux." They are almost always scams or cookie loggers designed to steal your account.

3

Consistency is Key

Luck is rare. Success comes from treating your Roblox presence like a small business. Keep creating and improving.
